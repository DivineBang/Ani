Functional collagen

There are several types of collagen and each of them has specific effects:

Type I: It is the most common in the body and is important for supporting healthy joints, bones and skin
Type II. : Mainly found in cartilages and helps maintain their elasticity and strength
Type III: Involved in the formation of skin, blood vessels and internal organs
Type IV: It is part of the basement membrane and helps maintain cell structure and function

Vegan collagen is created on a natural basis from plant sources instead of animal sources. It is made with plant proteins and other natural ingredients to provide support for healthy hair growth, joint and tissue health. Bovine collagen is type I collagen.
